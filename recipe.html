Callie Vege Tacos

ingredients

Soft flour tortillas

4

Avocado

2

Pickled red onions

1/2 cup

Red cabbage

1/2 cup

Feta cheese

1/3 cup

Cilantro leaves

Handful

Hot sauce (like siracha)

200ml

Steps

Place sliced onions in a bowl, then pour the warm pickling liquid over them

Let it sit for at least 30 minutes

Dice the avocado and chip the cilantro

Shred the red cabbage finely

Crumble the feta cheese

Warm tortillas in a pan

Layer ingredients onto the tortillas

Sprinkle the feta and fresh cilantro

Drizzle hot sauce all over

Serve open-faced for a taco-style presentation
